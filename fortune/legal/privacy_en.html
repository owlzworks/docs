Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Fortune Lab puts your privacy first. The birth details you enter (date, time, gender, name) are stored only on your device and are never sent to any server. The network is used solely to show ads and to check for app updates.

Information we handle

Birth details you enter for readings (date, time, gender, optional name) are saved only in local storage on your device. They are used solely to compute your saju, daily, new-year and compatibility readings, and are removed completely when you clear them or uninstall the app.

For the face-reading (관상) feature, photos you capture or select are analyzed entirely on your device using on-device face detection to locate facial regions. The photo is never uploaded, transmitted, or stored by the app; it is discarded after analysis.

The app also stores its own settings (language, appearance) locally.

There is no account and no sign-in; we operate no server that receives your data.

Advertising

The app shows ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect an advertising identifier and related data to serve and measure ads; see Google's policies.

Where required (e.g. EEA/UK), a consent form is shown and your choice can be changed later in Settings.
